of its full size, and lowered.

Make sure that marcam_icon55_layer.icns has all of the same size constituent
images as GenericDocumentIcon.icns 

build_scripts/blend_icns GenericDocumentIcon.icns marcam_icon55_layer.icns marcam_doc.icns
